simultaneity property

- solution of nth order differential equation equal solutions of $n$ 1st order differential equations
---
simultaneity property formula

$$
\begin{align*}
a_n(t)\frac{d^ny}{dt^n} + a_{n-1}(t)\frac{d^{n-1}y}{dt^{n-1}} + \dots + a_0y = 0 \rightarrow \begin{cases} \frac{dx_1}{dt} = x_2 \\
\frac{dx_{2}}{dt} = x_3 \\
\ \ \ \vdots \\
\frac{dx_{n-1}}{dt} = x_n \\
\frac{dx_{n}}{dt} = -(\frac{a_{n-1}(t)x_n + a_{n-2}(t)x_{n-1} + \dots + a_0x_1}{a_n(t)})
\end{cases} \\
x_1(t) = y \\
x_2(t) = \frac{dy}{dt} \\
\vdots \\
x_n(t) = \frac{d^{n-1}y}{dt^{n-1}}
\end{align*}
$$

linear dependence

- there exists nontrivial solution of homogeneous system
---
linear dependence formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Rank}(A) < n \rightarrow \vec x \ne \vec 0 \\
A = \text{square matrix} \\
n = \text{number of rows} \\
n = \text{number of columns} \\
\vec x = \text{solution}
\end{align*}
$$
---
linear independence

- every solution of homogeneous system equal trivial solution
---
linear independence formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Rank}(A) = n \rightarrow \vec x = \vec 0 \\
A = \text{square matrix} \\
n = \text{number of rows} \\
n = \text{number of columns} \\
\vec x = \text{solution}
\end{align*}
$$
---
span

- set of all possible linear combinations of the vectors of subspace
---
span formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Span}(A) = \{\sum_{i}^n c_i\vec a_i | c \in \mathbb R, \vec a \in \mathbb R^m\} \\
m = \text{number of rows} \\
n = \text{number of columns} \\
c = \text{scalar} \\
\vec a = \text{column vector} 
\end{align*}
$$

basis

- set of linearly independent vectors that span the vector space
---
basis property 

- any $n$ linearly independent vectors of $n$ dimensional vector space are the basis
- equal number of $n$ linearly independent vectors between every basis
---
dimension

- number of linearly independent vectors necessary for describing every possible vector of vector space
---
dimension property

- number of elements of basis equal the dimension of vector space
