random variable

- variable whose values equal the outcomes of random process
---
random variable formula

$$
\begin{align*}
X: \Omega \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\
X = \text{random variable} \\
\Omega = \text{sample space} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
continuous random variable

- infinite number of measurable values
---
discrete random variable formula

$$
\begin{align*}
(\{0, 1, 2, 3, \dots, n\} \not\sim X) \land (\mathbb N \not\sim X) \\
\end{align*}
$$
---
continuous probability density function

- probability as function of continuous random variable values
---
continuous PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall x \in X: f(x) \ge 0 \\
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x)dx = 1 \\
P(a \le X \le b) = \int_a^b f(x)dx = 1
\end{align*}
$$
---
continuous PDF mean formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\mu = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} xf(x)dx
\end{align*}
$$
---
continuous PDF standard deviation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\sigma = \sqrt{\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} (x - \mu)^2f(x)dx}
\end{align*}
$$
---
uniform probability density function

- probability as function of equally likely events
---
uniform PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x) = \frac{1}{b-a} \\
a = \text{lower endpoint} \\
b = \text{upper endpoint} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
uniform PDF mean formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\mu = \frac{a+b}{2} \\
a = \text{lower endpoint} \\
b = \text{upper endpoint} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
uniform PDF standard deviation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\sigma = \frac{b-a}{\sqrt {12}} \\
a = \text{lower endpoint} \\
b = \text{upper endpoint} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
exponential probability density function

- probability as function of time between events 
---
exponential PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x} \\
\lambda = \text{average number of events per time}
\end{align*}
$$
---
exponential PDF mean formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\mu = \frac{1}{\lambda} \\
\lambda = \text{average number of events per time}
\end{align*}
$$
---
exponential PDF standard deviation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\sigma = \frac{1}{\lambda} \\
\lambda = \text{average number of events per time}
\end{align*}
$$
---
normal probability density function

- probability as function of mean and standard deviation
---
normal PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
X \sim N(\mu, \sigma) \rightarrow f(x) = \frac{\exp(\frac{-(x - \mu)^2}{2\sigma^2})}{\sigma\sqrt {2\pi}} \\
\mu = \text{mean} \\
\sigma = \text{standard deviation}
\end{align*}
$$
---
standard normal probability density function

- probability as function of z score
---
standard normal PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
Z \sim N(0, 1) \rightarrow f(z) = \frac{e^{-z^2/2}}{\sqrt {2\pi}}
\end{align*}
$$
