bounded above

- there exist real number of set such that every element of set less or equal real number
---
bounded above formula

$$
\begin{align*}
S \subseteq \mathbb R, \exists u \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: x \le u \\
u = \text{upper bound} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
bounded below

- there exist real number of set such that every element of set greater or equal real number
---
bounded below formula

$$
\begin{align*}
S \subseteq \mathbb R,  \exists w  \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: w \le x \\
w = \text{lower bound} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
 bounded

- both bounded above and bounded below
---
bounded formula

$$
\begin{align*}
S \subseteq \mathbb R, \exists u, w \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: w \le x \le u \\
u = \text{upper bound} \\
w = \text{lower bound} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
unbounded

- either unbounded above or unbounded below
---
unbounded formula

$$
\begin{align*}
S \subseteq \mathbb R, \not\exists u, w \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: w \le x \le u \\
u = \text{upper bound} \\
w = \text{lower bound}
\end{align*}
$$
---
supremum

- least upper bound of bounded above set
---
supremum formula

$$
\begin{align*}
S \subseteq \mathbb R, \exists u \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: x \le u \le U \rightarrow u = \sup S\\
U = \{u, u+1, u+2, \dots, \infty\} \\
u = \text{supremum} \\
\end{align*}
$$
---
infimum

- greatest lower bound of bounded below set
---
infimum formula

$$
\begin{align*}
S \subseteq \mathbb R, \exists w \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: W \le w \le x \rightarrow w = \inf S = -\sup (-S)\\
W = \{w, w-1, w-2, \dots, -\infty\} \\
w = \text{infimum} \\
\end{align*}
$$
---
completeness property

- for every nonempty bounded above set there exists supremum
- for every nonempty bounded below set there exists infimum
---
completeness property formula

$$
\begin{align*}
S \subseteq \mathbb R, \exists u \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: x \le u \rightarrow \exists \sup S \\
S \subseteq \mathbb R, \exists w \in \mathbb R, \forall x \in S: w \le x \rightarrow \exists \inf S \\
u = \text{upper bound} \\
w = \text{lower bound} 
\end{align*}
$$

archimedean property

- natural numbers are unbounded above
- there does not exist smallest positive real number
---
archimedean property formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall (x > 0) \in \mathbb R, \forall y \in \mathbb R, \exists n \in \mathbb N: y < nx
\end{align*}
$$
---
density property

- between every two real numbers there exists rational number
---
density property

$$
\begin{align*}
(x, y \in \mathbb R) \land (x < y) \rightarrow \exists q \in \mathbb Q: x < q < y
\end{align*}
$$
---
positive nth root property

- for every positive real number there exists unique positive real number root for every natural number power
---
positive nth root property formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall (x > 0) \in \mathbb R, \exists !(y > 0) \in \mathbb R, \forall n \in \mathbb N: y^n = x \\
y = \text{positive nth root}
\end{align*}
$$
