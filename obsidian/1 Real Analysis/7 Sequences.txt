sequence

- list of ordered numbers
---
sequence formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\{a_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty} = a: \mathbb N \rightarrow S
\end{align*}
$$
---
convergent

- there exists limit of sequence
---
convergent formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}a_n = L \leftrightarrow \forall \epsilon > 0, \exists n_{\epsilon} \in \mathbb N: n \ge n_{\epsilon} \rightarrow d(a_n, L) < \epsilon
\end{align*}
$$
---
divergent

- there exists no limit of sequence
---
divergent formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}a_n \ne L \leftrightarrow (\exists \epsilon > 0, \forall n_{\epsilon} \in \mathbb N: n < n_{\epsilon}) \land (d(a_n, L) \ge \epsilon)
\end{align*}
$$
