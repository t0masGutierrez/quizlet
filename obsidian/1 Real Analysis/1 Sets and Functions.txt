cartesian product

- set of all 2-tuples from 2 sets
---
cartesian product formula

$$
\begin{align*}
X \times Y = \{(x, y) | x \in X, y \in Y\} \ne Y \times X
\end{align*}
$$
---
binary relation

- subset of cartesian product represent relationship between 2-tuples from two sets
---
binary relation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
(x, y) \in R \subset X \times Y \rightarrow xRy
\end{align*}
$$
---
equivalence relation

- reflexive
- symmetric
- transitive
---
equivalence relation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
(R \subset S \times S) \land (x \in S) \rightarrow x R x \\
(R \subset S \times S) \land (x, y \in S) \land (x R y) \rightarrow y R x \\
(R \subset S \times S) \land (x, y, z \in S) \land (x R y) \land (y R z) \rightarrow x R z 
\end{align*}
$$
---
equivalence class

- nonempty, disjoint subset consisting of all elements equivalent with representative under the equivalence relation
---
equivalence class formula

$$
\begin{align*}
[x] = \set{y \in S| yRx} \\
x = \text{representative} \\
R = \text{equivalence relation}
\end{align*}
$$
---
equivalence class property

- self
- equal
- disjoint
---
equivalence class property formula

$$
\begin{align*}
x \in [x] \\
xRy \rightarrow [x] = [y] \\
x\not R y \rightarrow [x] \cap [y] = \emptyset
\end{align*}
$$
---
function

- relation on cartesian product
---
function formula

$$
\begin{align*}
(aFb) \land (aFc) \rightarrow b = c \\
a \in A \rightarrow \exists b \in B: aFb 
\end{align*}
$$
---
injection

- every element of domain map to 1 element of codomain
- into
---
injection formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall a \in A, \forall b \in B: f(a) = f(b) \rightarrow a = b
\end{align*}
$$
---
surjection

- every element of codomain map to $\ge1$ element of domain
- onto
---
surjection formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall b \in B, \exists a \in A: f(a) = b
\end{align*}
$$
---
bijection

- every element of codomain map to 1 element of domain
- into and onto
---
bijection formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall b \in B, \exists !a \in A: f(a) = b
\end{align*}
$$
