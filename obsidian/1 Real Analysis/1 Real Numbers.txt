---
peano axioms

$$
\begin{align*}
0 \in \mathbb N \\
S(n) = n + 1 \rightarrow \forall n \in \mathbb N: S(n) \in \mathbb N \\
S(n) = n + 1 \rightarrow \forall n \not\in \mathbb N: S(n) \not\in \mathbb N \\
S(n) = S(m) \rightarrow n = m \\
(K \subseteq \mathbb N) \land (0 \in K) \land (\forall n \in K: S(n) \in K) \rightarrow K = \mathbb N
\end{align*}
$$

construct natural numbers

- initialize zero as the empty set
- successor equal the union of natural number with singleton set containing natural number
- natural numbers equal the set of successors
---
construct natural numbers formula

$$
\begin{align*}
0 &= \emptyset \\
1 &= S(0) = 0 \cup \{0\} = \{0\} \\
2 &= S(1) = 1 \cup \{1\} = \{0\} \cup \{1\} = \{0, 1\} \\
3 &= S(2) = 2 \cup \{2\} = \{0, 1\} \cup \{2\} = \{0, 1, 2\} \\
\vdots \\ 
\mathbb N &= \{S(n) | 0 \le n \le \infty\}
\end{align*}
$$

construct integers

- equivalence relation equal the tuple of natural pairs with equal difference
- equivalence class equal the set of equivalence relations with equal difference
- integers equal the set of equivalence classes
---
construct integers formula

$$
\begin{align*}
(m, n) \sim (m', n') \leftrightarrow m - n = m' - n' \\
[(m, n)] = \{(m', n') \in \mathbb N \times \mathbb N |(m, n) \sim (m', n')\} \\
\mathbb Z = \{[(m, n)] | (m, n) \in \mathbb N \times \mathbb N\}
\end{align*}
$$

field

- set with two binary operations that satisfy the 9 field axioms
---
field formula

$$
\begin{align*}
(+) : F \times F \rightarrow F \\
(\cdot ) : F \times F \rightarrow F
\end{align*}
$$
---
field axioms

- additive commutativity
- additive associativity
- additive identity
- additive inverse
- distributivity
- multiplicative commutativity
- multiplicative associativity
- multiplicative identity
- multiplicative inverse
---
field axioms formula

$$
\begin{align*}
x + y = y + x \\
x + (y + z) = (x + y) + z \\
x + (-x) = 0 \\
x + 0 = x \\
x\cdot (y + z) = xy + xz \\
x \cdot y = y \cdot x \\
x \cdot (y \cdot z) = (x \cdot y) \cdot z \\
x \cdot (\frac{1}{x}) = 1 \\
x \cdot 1 = x \\
\end{align*}
$$
---

- equality
- identity
- single inverse
- double inverse
---
field addition property formula

$$
\begin{align*}
x + y = x + z \rightarrow y = z \\
x + y = x \rightarrow y = 0 \\
x + y = 0 \rightarrow y = -x \\
-(-x) = x
\end{align*}
$$

- zero
- nonzero
- single negative
- double negative
- equality

---
field multiplication property formula

$$
\begin{align*}
0x = 0 \\
(x \ne 0) \land (y \ne 0) \rightarrow xy \ne 0 \\
(-x)y = -(xy) = x(-y) \\
(-x)(-y) = xy
(x \ne 0) \land (xy = xz) \rightarrow y = z \\

x \ne 0 \rightarrow \frac{1}{1/x} = x \\
\end{align*}
