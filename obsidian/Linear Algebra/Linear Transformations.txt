function

- map every element from A to 1 element of B
---
function formula

$$
\begin{align*}
f: A \to B \\
f(a) = b
\end{align*}
$$
---
domain

- if $f$ equal function from A to B then A is domain of $f$ 
---
codomain

- if $f$ equal function from A to B then B is codomain of $f$ 
---
preimage

- if $f(a) = b$ then *a* equal preimage of *b* 
---
image

- if $f(a) = b$ then *b* equal image of *a* 
---
range

- if $f$ equal function from A to B then subset of B equal range of $f$ 
---
linear transformation

- function of vector space thats closed under vector addition and scalar multiplication
---
linear transformation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
L: \mathcal V \rightarrow \mathcal W \\
L(\vec v) = \vec w \\
f(\vec v_1 + \vec v_2) = f(\vec v_1) + f(\vec v_2) \\
f(c\vec v) = cf(\vec v)
\end{align*}
$$
---
linear operator

- linear transformation whose domain equal codomain
---
linear operator formula

$$
\begin{align*}
f : \mathcal V \rightarrow \mathcal V \\
f = \text{function} \\
\mathcal V = \text{vector space}
\end{align*}
$$
---
identity linear operator

- linear transformation whose preimage equal image
---
identity linear operator formula

$$
\begin{align*}
i : \mathcal V \rightarrow \mathcal V \\
f(\vec v) = \vec v
\end{align*}
$$
---
translation

- translate vector along subspace but not linear operator
---
translation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
[v_1, \dots, v_i, \dots, v_n] \mapsto [v_1, \dots, v_i + c, \dots, v_n]
\end{align*}
$$
---
reflection

- reflect vector across subspace
---
reflection formula

$$
\begin{align*}
[v_1, \dots, v_i, \dots, v_n] \mapsto [v_1, \dots, -v_i, \dots, v_n]
\end{align*}
$$
---
contraction

- decrease vector along subspace
---
contraction formula

$$
\begin{align*}
[v_1, \dots, v_i, \dots, v_n] \mapsto [v_1, \dots, cv_i, \dots, v_n] \\
0 \le c \le 1
\end{align*}
$$
---
dilation

- increase vector along subspace
---
dilation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
[v_1, \dots, v_i, \dots, v_n] \mapsto [v_1, \dots, cv_i, \dots, v_n] \\
c > 1
\end{align*}
$$
---
projection

- project vector onto subspace
---
projection formula

$$
\begin{align*}
[v_1, \dots, v_i, \dots, v_n] \mapsto [v_1, \dots, 0, \dots, v_n]
\end{align*}
$$
---
rotation

- rotate vector about origin
---
rotation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
[v_1, \dots, v_i, \dots, v_n] \mapsto \begin{bmatrix} \cos \theta & -\sin \theta \\ \sin \theta & \cos \theta \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} v_1 \\ \vdots \\ v_i \\ \vdots \\ v_n \end{bmatrix} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
multiplication linear transformation

- map every element from $\mathbb R^n$ to 1 element of $\mathbb R^m$ 
---
multiplication linear transformation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
f: \mathbb R^n \rightarrow \mathbb R^m \\
f(\vec x) = A\vec x
\end{align*}
$$
---
linear transformation property

- zero
- negation
- linear combination
---
linear transformation property

$$
\begin{align*}
L(\vec 0_{\mathcal V}) = \vec 0_{\mathcal W} \\
L(-\vec v) = -\vec v \\
L(c_1\vec v_1 + \dots + c_n\vec v_n) = c_1L(\vec v_1) + \dots + c_nL(\vec v_n) \\
\end{align*}
$$
---
composite linear transformation

- composition of linear transformation equal linear transformation 
---
composite linear transformation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
L_2 \circ L_1 : \mathcal V_1 \rightarrow \mathcal V_3 \\
(L_2 \circ L_1)(\vec v) = L_2(L_1(\vec v))
\end{align*}
$$
---
subspace linear transformation

- image of domain subspace equal subspace of codomain
- preimage of codomain subspace equal subspace of domain
---
subspace linear transformation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\mathcal V\prime \le \mathcal V \rightarrow L(\mathcal V\prime) = \{L(\vec v) | \vec v \in \mathcal V\prime\} \le \mathcal W \\
\mathcal W\prime \le \mathcal W \rightarrow L^{-1}(\mathcal W\prime) = \{\vec v | L(\vec v) \in \mathcal W\prime\} \le \mathcal V
\end{align*}
$$
