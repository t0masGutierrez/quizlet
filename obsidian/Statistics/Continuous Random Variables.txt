random variable

- variable whose values equal the outcomes of random process
---
random variable formula

$$
\begin{align*}
X: \Omega \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\
X = \text{random variable} \\
\Omega = \text{sample space} \\
\mathbb{R} = \text{real number}
\end{align*}
$$
---
continuous random variable

- infinite number of measurable values
---
continuous probability density function

- probability as function of continuous random variable values
---
continuous PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\forall x f(x)\ge 0 \\
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x)dx = 1
\end{align*}
$$
---
continuous PDF mean formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\mu = \int xf(x)dx
\end{align*}
$$
---
continuous PDF standard deviation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\sigma = \sqrt{\int (x - \mu)^2f(x)dx}
\end{align*}
$$
---
cumulative probability density function

- area under curve equal probability
---
cumulative PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\int_a^bf(x)dx = P(a \le X \le b)
\end{align*}
$$
---
uniform probability density function

- probability as function of equally likely events
---
uniform PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x) = \frac{1}{b-a} \\
a = \text{lower endpoint} \\
b = \text{upper endpoint} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
uniform PDF mean formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\mu = \frac{a+b}{2} \\
a = \text{lower endpoint} \\
b = \text{upper endpoint} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
uniform PDF standard deviation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\sigma = \sqrt {\frac{(b-a)^2}{12}} \\
a = \text{lower endpoint} \\
b = \text{upper endpoint} 
\end{align*}
$$
---
exponential probability density function

- probability as function of time between poisson events 
---
exponential PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
f(x) = \beta e^{-\beta x} \\
\beta = \text{decay rate}
\end{align*}
$$
---
exponential PDF mean formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\mu = \frac{1}{\beta} \\
\beta = \text{decay rate}
\end{align*}
$$
---
exponential PDF standard deviation formula

$$
\begin{align*}
\sigma = \frac{1}{\beta} \\
\beta = \text{decay rate}
\end{align*}
$$
---
normal probability distribution function

- bell shaped, unimodal, symmetric, and continuous curve
---
normal PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
X \sim N(\mu, \sigma) \\
\mu = \text{mean} \\
\sigma = \text{standard deviation}
\end{align*}
$$
---
standard normal probability distribution function

- probability as function of z score
---
standard normal PDF formula

$$
\begin{align*}
Z \sim N(0, 1)
\end{align*}
$$
---
z score

- number of standard deviations from the mean
---
z score formula

$$
\begin{align*}
z = \frac{x - \mu}{\sigma} \\
\mu = \text{mean} \\
\sigma = \text{standard deviation}
\end{align*}
$$
